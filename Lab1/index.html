HUR Airlines is an Indian low-cost airline headquartered in Kurnool,Hyderabad,Chennai,Mumbai.HUR Airlines focuses on providing international flights from Tier II cities in India, to the Arab Countries , America and South East Asia. The airline flies to 47 destinations as of May 2022 with a primary hub located at Uyyalawada Narasimha Reddy Airport in Kurnool.On the domestic front, the airline operates from the states of Kerala, Delhi, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Telangana and Maharashtra. In its international arena, the airline mainly connects Indian cities with Gulf countries, America and Singapore in the east.
